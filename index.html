When you round the corner into an enemy, you spray. When you are in a 3v1 situation, you spray. When you think you saw an enemy (but you didn't), you spray. Taking aim usually keeps you stationary, leaving you vulnerable. When spraying, try to rotate around the enemy keeping short distance and let loose on the trigger. 3/4 of the time you'll get the kill. It's hard to avoid being hit by a bullet if someone sprays you down. The only thing you'll have to worry about is how many bullets you have in the clip afterwards, which is very few. Always run before your teammate to lower the enemies' health and distract them from your teammates to keep them alive.
