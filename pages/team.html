About Us

Our team may be small, but we know what we're doing. We come from around the US and Canada. We're always looking for new players that want to be a part of our team. We've all met in-game and play regularly. If you want to be a part of this team, go ahead and request to join*, there's a high chance you'll be accepted. It's fun to be with the team, and we're proud to be a part of it. To stay connected to upcoming events and community map games, join our Discord server. Also follow/subscribe to us on Twitch and YouTube.

*Must be Steam level 1 or higher and play CS:GO.
